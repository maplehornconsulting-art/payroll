🍁 CRA Payroll Tax Feed

A free, open, machine-readable feed of Canadian federal and provincial payroll tax data, published by MapleHorn Consulting Inc. and hosted on GitHub Pages. Updated automatically from CRA source pages via GitHub Actions.

⚠ Disclaimer: This feed is not an official product of the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA) or the Government of Canada. Values are derived from publicly available CRA publications and are provided for convenience only. All payroll calculations must be reviewed and approved by a qualified Canadian payroll professional. No warranty of any kind is provided.

Available Endpoints

FileDescription
v1/ca/latest.json Most-recent published values (always up to date)
v1/ca/index.json Catalogue of all dated releases with SHA-256 checksums
v1/ca/<effective_date>.json Snapshot for a specific CRA effective date (e.g. 2026-07-01.json)
v1/ca/<effective_date>.json.sig Detached GPG signature (present only when signing key is configured)

Fetch & Verify

# Fetch the latest feed
curl -fsSL https://maplehornconsulting-art.github.io/payroll/v1/ca/latest.json \
     -o latest.json

# (Optional) Verify GPG signature
curl -fsSL https://maplehornconsulting-art.github.io/payroll/v1/ca/latest.json.sig \
     -o latest.json.sig
gpg --verify latest.json.sig latest.json
